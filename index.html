Summary

In the past two weeks, we successfully built a ray tracer simulating the visual appearance of black holes and their surroundings based on the principles of general relativity. The Schwarzschild geometrical model we employed describes the spacetime around a non-rotating, spherically symmetric mass, where the Schwarzschild radius and the speed of light both equal one. We also employed CPU multiprocessing to utilize multiple cores for faster rendering.

Features we successfully simulated:
